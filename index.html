Welcome to Baby Steps!

Baby Steps is a growth tracking website for parents, by parents.

Baby Steps is a website used to keep track of your baby's vital growth data.

Monitoring your baby's growth can be vital in determining if everything is growing normally. Keeping track of these measurements will help you in deciding if there are questions to bring up with your pediatrician. With Baby Steps, you can record and track your baby's weight, length, and head circumference for your own reference or for your doctors.

You can even make journal entries to keep track of baby's important milestones or questions for the next check-up!
